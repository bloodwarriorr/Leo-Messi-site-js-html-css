CAMPEONES DE AMÉRICA

LEO CLAIMS COPA AMÉRICA CROWN & PLAYER OF TOURNAMENT AWARD

Leo Messi lifted the Copa América trophy after Argentina overcame Brazil in a 1-0 win in the tournament showpiece at the Maracana Stadium!

The Albiceleste captain was named Player of the Tournament after finishing top of the goals (four) and assists (five) charts. This trophy triumph represents the third of Leo's career with the Argentina national team after he captured gold at the 2008 Olympic Games and won the 2005 U-20 World Cup.


Achievements


Leo Earms 150TH Argentina Cap

Leo Messi clocked up his 150th appearance in an Argentina shirt in the Copa América semi-final clash against Colombia. Leo currently tops the competition's goals (four) and assists charts (five).

Breaking Records


Ten Years Since Leo Was Declared Historical Sports Heritage Of Humanity

This Tuesday marks the 10th anniversary since Leo Messi was named Historical Sports Heritage of Humanity by the International Bureau of Cultural Capitals after he prevailed in voting that involved 72 states and over 300,000 voters.

Thropies


Leo Claims Eight Pichichi Trophy

Leo Messi has finished the LaLiga season as the division's leading marksman, courtesy of his 30-goal haul. This is the eighth time that the Argentine has secured the Pichichi Trophy, a tally that no other player has achieved, whilst this is the fifth season in a row that he has has clinched the gong.
